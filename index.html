🤲 Duas for Ramadan

Daily Ramadan Dua

اللَّهُمَّ أَعِنِّي عَلَى ذِكْرِكَ وَشُكْرِكَ وَحُسْنِ عِبَادَتِكَ
O Allah, help me to remember You, to thank You, and to worship You in the best of ways.

Sehri Dua

نَوَيْتُ أَنْ أَصُومَ غَدًا عَنْ أَدَاءِ فَرْضِ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ هَذِهِ السَّنَةِ لِلَّهِ تَعَالَى
I intend to keep the fast for tomorrow in the month of Ramadan this year for the sake of Allah, the Most High.

Iftar Dua

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ آمَنْتُ وَعَلَى رِزْقِكَ أَفْطَرْتُ
O Allah, I fasted for You, and I believe in You, and I break my fast with Your sustenance.

Laylatul Qadr Dua

اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عُفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي
O Allah, You are Forgiving and love forgiveness, so forgive me.
